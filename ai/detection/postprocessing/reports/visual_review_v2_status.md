# OB-FVG Pairing v2 Visual Review

## Status
PASS

## Summary
The top 10 OB-FVG candidates generated by post-processing v2 were visually reviewed. Most HIGH and MEDIUM quality candidates show reasonable OB-FVG pairing around impulsive market movements.

## Reviewed Output
- Source: ob_fvg_pairs_v2.csv
- Visual folder: ai/detection/postprocessing/visual_review/top_pairs_v2
- Reviewed candidates: Top 10 ranked pairs

## Result
- HIGH quality pairs visually appear strong and structurally reasonable.
- MEDIUM quality pairs are mostly acceptable but require additional validation.
- The pairing method successfully reduces raw YOLO detections into cleaner setup candidates.
- Some candidates still require OHLCV-based direction validation.

## Key Observations
1. OB and FVG boxes are usually close and structurally related.
2. HIGH quality candidates generally appear around impulsive moves.
3. Some MEDIUM candidates may be directionally ambiguous.
4. Image-coordinate direction estimation is useful for early filtering but not sufficient for final decision-making.

## Conclusion
Pairing v2 is accepted as a valid baseline post-processing stage. The next improvement should validate direction and setup structure using OHLCV candle data instead of relying only on visual coordinates.

## Next Step

- Match prediction file names to source OHLCV windows.
- Validate whether the detected OB-FVG pair is bullish or bearish based on candle structure.
